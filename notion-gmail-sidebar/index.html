FAQ

Common questions.

Does this work with any Notion database?

Yes. As long as your database has an email property, the sidebar can match contacts from Gmail. You pick which database and which fields to display during setup.

Is my Notion data secure?

Your Notion API token is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage. It never passes through our servers — lookups happen directly from your browser to Notion's API.

Do I need to be technical to set it up?

No. The setup wizard walks you through connecting your Notion workspace and picking your fields. It takes about 5 minutes and requires no coding.

What happens if a contact isn't in my Notion database?

The sidebar stays out of your way. It only appears when it finds a matching record — no noise for unrecognized senders.
